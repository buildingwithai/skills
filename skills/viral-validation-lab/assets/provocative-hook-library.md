# Provocative Hook Library

100+ hook templates organized by controversy level.

## Tier 1: Safe Hooks (30-40% Controversy)

1. "The [Number] [Thing] that [Outcome]"
2. "How to [Desirable Result] in [Time Period]"
3. "Why [Common Action] doesn't work (and what does)"
4. "The truth about [Topic]"
5. "[Role/Expert] reveals [Secret]"

## Tier 2: Spicy Hooks (60-75% Controversy)

**Authority Challenge:**
6. "[Authority Figure] said this was [Negative]. Now I [Positive Outcome]."
7. "Why [Expert/Teacher/Boss] was wrong about [Topic]"
8. "I ignored [Common Advice]. Here's what happened."

**Bold Decisions:**
9. "Why we [Controversial Decision]"
10. "We [Unexpected Action] and [Surprising Result]"
11. "I quit [Safe Choice] to [Risky Choice]. No regrets."

**Category Attacks:**
12. "[Popular Tool/Method] is making you [Negative Outcome]"
13. "Why everyone uses [Thing] wrong"
14. "The [Industry] doesn't want you to know this"

**Underdog Stories:**
15. "They said I'd fail. I made [$Amount/Achievement]."
16. "Rejected by [Authority]. Now I [Success]."
17. "Started with [$Small Amount]. Now at [$Large Amount]."

## Tier 3: Nuclear Hooks (80-100% Controversy)

**Forbidden Angle:**
18. "[Platform/Authority] banned/deleted this. We [Did It Anyway]."
19. "The [Feature/Product] they don't want you to have"
20. "This got me [Negative Consequence]. Worth it."

**Extreme Claims:**
21. "[Absurd Statement]. Here's the proof."
22. "I [Extreme Action]. The results shocked me."
23. "[Controversial Opinion]. Fight me in the comments."

**Taboo Topics:**
24. "Let's talk about [Taboo Topic] nobody discusses"
25. "I [Socially Unacceptable Action] for [Time Period]"
26. "The truth about [Taboo Topic]: [Controversial Take]"

**Provocative Comparisons:**
27. "[Unexpected Thing 1] is better than [Expected Thing 2]"
28. "Why [Taboo Topic] matters more than [Accepted Topic]"
29. "I chose [Unconventional] over [Conventional]. Here's why."

**Call-Outs:**
30. "If you [Common Behavior], you're [Negative Label]"
31. "Stop [Popular Action]. You're doing it wrong."
32. "Your [Tool/Method/Belief] is the problem"

## Format Variations

**Question Hooks:**
33. "What if [Provocative Scenario]?"
34. "Why does [Absurd Situation] exist?"
35. "Who actually [Controversial Action]?"

**Statement + Contradiction:**
36. "Everyone says [Common Belief]. They're wrong."
37. "You think [X]. Actually, [Opposite of X]."
38. "I used to believe [Y]. Then I learned [Opposite]."

**Time-based:**
39. "Day 1 vs Day [Number] of [Challenge/Transformation]"
40. "I [Action] for [Time Period]. Results:"
41. "[X] years ago vs today: [Comparison]"

**Numbers/Stats:**
42. "I analyzed [Large Number]. Here's what I found."
43. "[Percentage]% of people don't know this"
44. "$[Amount] later, here's what I learned"

**Behind-the-Scenes:**
45. "How we really [Do Thing] (not what we tell you)"
46. "What [Industry] looks like behind closed doors"
47. "The [Process] nobody shows you"

## Usage Guide

**Select by:**
1. Your controversy dial setting (60-100%)
2. Your industry constraints
3. What fits your positioning
4. What can you back up with data/story

**Customize:**
- Replace [brackets] with your specifics
- Add your unique angle
- Match to your brand voice
- Ensure you can defend the claim

**Test:**
- Try 3 different hook types
- Measure completion rate
- Double down on winner
