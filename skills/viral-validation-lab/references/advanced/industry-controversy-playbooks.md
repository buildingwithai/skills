# Industry Controversy Playbooks

Quick reference for pushing boundaries by industry type.

## B2B SaaS (60% Controversy)

**Tactics:**
- Attack category leader: "Slack is making you dumber"
- Expose "secrets": "What [tool] doesn't tell you"
- Bold customer decisions: "We fired our best customer"
- Challenge norms: "Why we banned meetings"

**Examples:** See [b2b-saas-boring-product-example.md](../examples/b2b-saas-boring-product-example.md)

## Consumer/Taboo Products (80-100%)

**Tactics:**
- Forbidden angle: "X banned this"
- Embrace absurdity: Take taboo seriously
- Double meanings: Playful innuendo
- Normalization: Community-building angle

**Examples:** See [foot-fetish-app-example.md](../examples/foot-fetish-app-example.md)

## Music/Entertainment (95%)

**Tactics:**
- Break industry "rules": "My teacher said not to"
- Genre-bending controversy: Mix unexpected styles
- Cultural boundary-pushing: Challenge regional norms
- Underdog narrative: Prove doubters wrong

**Examples:** See [bilingual-artist-example.md](../examples/bilingual-artist-example.md)
