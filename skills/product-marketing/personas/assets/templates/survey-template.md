## Analysis Guide

### Step 1: Frequency Analysis
For each question, calculate:
- % selecting each option
- Most common responses
- Patterns by segment (company size, role)

### Step 2: Validate Interview Insights

**Example:**
```
Interview insight: "Sales Managers triggered by lost deals"
Survey Q3: 67% selected "Lost an important deal" ✅ VALIDATED

Interview insight: "Mobile app is critical"
Survey Q11: Only 23% said "must-have" ❌ REJECTED
```

### Step 3: Segment Analysis

Compare responses by:
- Role (P1 vs P2 vs P3)
- Company size (SMB vs Enterprise)
- Industry

**Look for:** Do different segments have different priorities?

### Step 4: Update Personas

Add data-driven insights:
```
BEFORE: "Sales Managers care about ROI"
AFTER: "Sales Managers care about ROI (ranked #1 by 78% in survey of 200 managers)"
```

## Success Metrics

**Good survey:**
- 200+ responses
- 5-8% response rate from email list
- 80%+ completion rate (not too long)
- <5 min average completion time
- Representative sample across segments

**Validation threshold:**
- 60%+ confirm interview insights → Validated
- 40-59% → Uncertain, need more research
- <40% → Rejected, update persona
