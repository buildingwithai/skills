## Validation Score

Calculate validation confidence:

**Formula:**
```
Validation Score = (# Validated Assumptions) / (Total Assumptions) √ó 100
```

**Example:**
```
15 validated / 20 total = 75% validation score
```

**Interpretation:**
- **90%+:** High confidence persona
- **75-89%:** Good confidence, minor refinements needed
- **60-74%:** Moderate confidence, needs more validation
- **<60%:** Low confidence, significant research needed

Based on validation results:

**If mostly validated (75%+):**
‚Üí Activate persona in campaigns

‚Üí Use confidently in messaging

**If mixed results (50-75%):**
‚Üí Focus validation efforts on uncertain areas

‚Üí Hold off on major activation until more confident

**If mostly rejected (<50%):**
