# Persona Activation Checklist

> **TEMPLATE:** Use this to roll out personas across your organization

## Pre-Launch Prep

### Persona Finalization
- [ ] All personas validated with customer data
- [ ] Formatted in shareable format (PDF, slides, one-pagers)
- [ ] Executive summary created (1-page overview of all personas)
- [ ] Quick reference cards created (wallet-sized)

### Distribution Materials
- [ ] Persona one-pagers (print & digital)
- [ ] Persona slide deck (for presentations)
- [ ] Poster versions (for office walls)
- [ ] Digital versions (PDF, web, intranet)
- [ ] Quick reference guide (cheat sheet)

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Low Adoption
**Symptoms:** Personas not being used, fields not filled
**Fixes:**
- [ ] Get executive reinforcement
- [ ] Make it mandatory (tie to processes)
- [ ] Show quick wins from teams using them
- [ ] Simplify (too complex?)

### Problem: Confusion
**Symptoms:** Teams unsure which persona applies, conflicting interpretations
**Fixes:**
- [ ] Create decision tree for persona selection
- [ ] Add examples to each persona
- [ ] Hold office hours for questions
- [ ] Clarify differentiation between personas

### Problem: Disagreement
**Symptoms:** Teams say personas don't match reality
**Fixes:**
- [ ] Show research that backs personas
- [ ] Interview team to understand gap
- [ ] May need to update personas
- [ ] Check if team talking to right customers

### Problem: Stale Personas
**Symptoms:** Personas feel outdated, market has changed
**Fixes:**
- [ ] Schedule refresh (interviews + surveys)
- [ ] Update quarterly with new insights
- [ ] Mark assumptions that need revalidation
- [ ] Retire outdated personas
