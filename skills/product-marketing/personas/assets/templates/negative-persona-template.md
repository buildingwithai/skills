### Sales Qualification

**Disqualification criteria:**
Add to BANT/MEDDIC:
- [ ] Company size must be _____+ employees
- [ ] Budget minimum: $_____
- [ ] Role must be: _____________________
- [ ] Use case must be: _____________________
- [ ] NOT a [negative persona type]

**Qualification script:**
```
"Before we continue, I want to make sure we're a good fit...

- What's your company size? [If <X employees → Disqualify]
- What's your role? [If student/researcher → Disqualify]
- What problem are you trying to solve? [If wrong use case → Disqualify]
- What's your timeline for a decision? [If "just researching" → Disqualify]
"
```

**Disqualification message:**
```
"Thanks for your interest! Based on what you've shared, I don't think we're the
best fit for your needs. Here are some alternatives that might work better:
[COMPETITOR 1], [COMPETITOR 2]"
```
