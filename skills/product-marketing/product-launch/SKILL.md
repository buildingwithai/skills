## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need to:

- **Plan product launches** - 90-day roadmaps, launch tiers, resource allocation
- **Execute launches** - Day-of coordination, multi-channel tactics
- **Coordinate teams** - Cross-functional RACI, stakeholder management

**NOT for:**
- Positioning (use `positioning` skill - PREREQUISITE)
- Messaging (use `messaging` skill - PREREQUISITE)

## Key Principles

### 1. Positioning and Messaging Are Prerequisites

Cannot launch without:
- Clear positioning (who you're for, why different)
- Validated messaging (value props that resonate)

**95% of products fail due to insufficient market understanding.**

### 2. Launch Tiers Prevent Burnout

Not all launches deserve equal resources:
- **Tier 1:** Max 2 per quarter, full resources
- **Tier 2:** 4-8 per quarter, selective resources
- **Tier 3:** Ongoing, minimal resources

### 3. First 48 Hours Matter Most

Launch day requires:
- War room intensity
- Hour-by-hour playbook
- Real-time monitoring
- Crisis protocols

### 4. Post-Launch is Where Work Begins

Week 1 feedback reveals what planning missed:
- 2-week sprint cycles
- Tight feedback loops
- AARRR metrics
