## Pre-Meeting Preparation (1 Week Before)

### Data Collection

- [ ] **Pull quarterly metrics**
  - Pricing page traffic
  - Conversion rate by tier
  - ACV (Average Contract Value)
  - MRR/ARR growth
  - NRR (Net Revenue Retention)
  - Churn rate
  - CAC payback period
  - Tier distribution
  - Annual vs monthly mix
  - Free-to-paid conversion (if freemium)

- [ ] **Segment analysis**
  - SMB vs mid-market vs enterprise
  - Geography (US, EU, Asia, etc.)
  - Channel (organic, paid, referral, sales-led)
  - Cohort trends (newer customers vs older)

- [ ] **Competitive intel**
  - Competitor pricing changes this quarter
  - New entrants to market
  - Win/loss analysis (pricing as factor)

- [ ] **Customer feedback synthesis**
  - Support tickets re: pricing
  - Sales objections (top 5)
  - Churn reasons (price-related)
  - NPS comments (pricing mentions)

- [ ] **Experiment results**
  - Active pricing tests this quarter
  - Results (statistically significant?)
  - Recommendations (implement/reject/iterate)

## Meeting Agenda (90 Minutes)

### Part 1: Performance Review (30 minutes)

#### Q1: Quarterly Metrics vs Targets

- [ ] **Conversion rate**
  - Target: ___%
  - Actual: ___%
  - Variance: ___% (above/below)
  - Trend: Improving / Stable / Declining

**Discussion:**
- Why variance? (market, competition, product changes)
- Action needed: Yes / No

---

- [ ] **ACV (Average Contract Value)**
  - Target: $_____
  - Actual: $_____
  - Variance: $_____
  - Trend: Improving / Stable / Declining

**Discussion:**
- Are customers buying higher tiers? (expansion)
- Segment differences: SMB vs enterprise

---

- [ ] **NRR (Net Revenue Retention)**
  - Target: 115-125% (high-growth benchmark)
  - Actual: ___%
  - Variance: ___% points
  - Trend: Improving / Stable / Declining

**Discussion:**
- Expansion vs contraction vs churn breakdown
- Pricing model supporting expansion? (usage growth, tier upgrades)

---

- [ ] **Churn rate**
  - Target: <___% monthly
  - Actual: ___%
  - Variance: ___% points
  - Price-related churn: ___%

**Discussion:**
- Is price a top churn reason?
- Specific cohorts churning more (recent price increase?)

---

- [ ] **Tier distribution**
  - Free: ___% (target: ___)
  - Tier 1: ___% (target: ___)
  - Tier 2: ___% (target: 60-70%)
  - Tier 3: ___% (target: ___)
  - Enterprise: ___% (target: ___)

**Discussion:**
- Is target tier (Tier 2) most popular?
- Any tiers underperforming? (re-package?)

---

- [ ] **Annual vs monthly mix**
  - Target: ___% annual
  - Actual: ___% annual
  - Trend: Improving / Stable / Declining

**Discussion:**
- Is annual discount compelling enough?
- Need to increase incentive?

### Red Flags (Immediate Action Required)

**Trigger immediate review if:**
- ❌ Conversion drops >10% for 2+ weeks
- ❌ Churn spikes >2x normal for 2+ weeks
- ❌ Competitor launches major pricing change
- ❌ Major customer threat to churn due to price
- ❌ Product launch requires pricing change

**Escalation:** Convene Monetization Council ad-hoc
