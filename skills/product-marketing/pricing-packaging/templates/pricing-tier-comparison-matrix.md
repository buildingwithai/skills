## Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring

**Highest tier (Tier 3) anchors others:**
- Tier 3 at $_____ makes Tier 2 at $_____ feel like a bargain

**Recommended tier positioning:**
- Highlight Tier 2 as "Most Popular" or "Best Value"
- Badge, color, slight elevation on pricing page

---

### Decoy Effect

**Tier 1 serves as "decoy" to make Tier 2 attractive:**
- Tier 1: $_____ (limited features)
- Tier 2: $_____ (significantly more features for slightly higher price)
- Psychological push: "For just $_____ more, get _____"

---

### Good/Better/Best

**Three paid tiers (+ free + enterprise):**
- Good (Tier 1): Acceptable, but limited
- **Better (Tier 2): Obvious choice** ⭐
- Best (Tier 3): Premium, but overkill for most

**Target distribution:**
- Tier 1: 15-20%
- Tier 2: 60-70% ⭐
- Tier 3: 15-20%
- Enterprise: 5-10%
