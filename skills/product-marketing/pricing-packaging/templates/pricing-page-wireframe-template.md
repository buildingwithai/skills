- Collapsible FAQ
- Touch-friendly buttons (44px minimum height)

## Copy Guidelines

### Tier Names

**Avoid:**
- Generic: "Basic", "Standard", "Advanced"
- Confusing: "Silver", "Gold", "Platinum"

**Better:**
- Audience-focused: "Solo", "Team", "Company", "Enterprise"
- Use case-focused: "Starter", "Professional", "Business"
- Value-focused: "Essential", "Plus", "Premium"

---

### Taglines

**Formula:** "For [audience] that need [key benefit]"

**Examples:**
- "For individuals getting started"
- "For growing teams that need collaboration"
- "For companies with advanced needs"
- "For enterprises requiring custom solutions"

---

### Feature Copy

**Not:** "API access" (feature)
**Better:** "API access to integrate with your existing tools" (benefit)

**Not:** "99.99% uptime SLA" (technical)
**Better:** "Your team can rely on always being online" (benefit)

**Events to track:**
- Page load

- Tier card click
- CTA button click (by tier)

## A/B Testing Ideas

- "Get Started" vs "Start Free Trial" vs "Try Free for 14 Days"

**Test 2: Tier order**
- Low-to-high vs High-to-low

**Test 3: Highlighted tier**
- Tier 2 vs Tier 3 as "Most Popular"

**Test 4: Annual discount messaging**
- "Save 20%" vs "Get 2 months free" vs "Save $240/year"
