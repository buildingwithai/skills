### Production Notes:

**Filming:**
- Shot on iPhone (authentic feel)

## Key Learnings

### What Worked:

1. **Forbidden Angle:** "Tinder banned this" was THE hook
2. **Normalizing Taboo:** Treating seriously + humor balance
3. **Community Framing:** "1 in 7 people" = you're not alone
4. **Double Meanings:** "TASTE" kept it playful
5. **Visual Simplicity:** iPhone, quick cuts, no over-production

### What to Replicate:

- Always lead with controversy (don't bury it)
- Use "banned/forbidden" angles when possible
- Mix seriousness with absurdity
- Create community feeling (not isolating)
- Keep production authentic (over-polish kills relatability)

### Metrics That Mattered Most:

1. **Completion rate** (78%) - Proved hook worked
2. **Share rate** (7.8%) - Proved viral potential
3. **Link clicks** (4.2K) - Proved intent to use product
4. **Comment debate** - Proved healthy controversy
