# Competitive Content Intelligence

Use `competitive-intelligence` skill but focus on:

## What to Research

1. **Content Formats:** What's working for competitors?
2. **Avoided Topics:** What are they NOT saying? (Opportunity)
3. **Engagement Patterns:** Which posts get most shares?
4. **Platform Presence:** Where are they active/absent?

## Tools

- TikTok: Search competitor name, check "Top" videos
- YouTube: Sort by "Most Popular"
- Instagram: Check Reels tab, sort by plays
- Social listening: Track competitor mentions

## Extract for Content Strategy

**Gaps = Opportunities:**
- Topics competitors avoid → Your controversy angle
- Formats no one's using → Your unique approach
- Platforms they ignore → Your distribution advantage

## Integration

Call `competitive-intelligence` skill → Content Analysis sections only
Skip: Battle cards, win/loss (unless mining for content)
