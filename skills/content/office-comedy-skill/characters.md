## 1. JOVANNY - Founder & CEO
**Office Archetype:** Michael Scott

### Basic Info
- **Age:** 32
- **Role:** Founder & CEO of QuickPing
- **Physical Description for Sora:**
  - Height: 6'4" (notably tall, this should be visible in frame - he towers over others)
  - Build: Athletic, gym-bro physique (broad shoulders, defined arms)
  - Typical outfit: Fitted henley shirts or quarter-zips that show physique, startup casual, sometimes QuickPing hoodie
  - Hair: Well-maintained, modern cut
  - Energy: High-energy body language, uses hands when talking, takes up space

### Core Blind Spots
1. **ADHD/Terrible Prioritization** - Starts on one thing, goes down rabbit holes, forgets original task. Will derail meetings to discuss tangents.
2. **Cannot Take Constructive Feedback** - Gets defensive immediately, "Well I think it's actually great," turns it around
3. **Desperately Wants to Be Liked** - Especially by younger employees, tries too hard to be "cool"
4. **Lacks Self-Awareness** - Doesn't realize when he's being inappropriate or cringe

### Verbal Tics
- "Bro" constantly, especially with Marcus
- "That's actually really interesting because..." (deflecting feedback)
- "I was just thinking about this..." (went down a rabbit hole)
- References his gym routine unprompted
- "We're like a family here"
- Misuses startup jargon confidently

### Physical Signature
- **The Height Thing:** People's first reaction meeting him in person is always "Whoa, you're tall." He's used to it, just nods "Yeah, 6'4""
- Stands too close to people (doesn't realize his size is intimidating)
- Gym bro posture - chest out, shoulders back
- Tries to dap up younger employees, comes off awkward

### Key Relationships
- **Wei:** His "Toby" - Wei's deadpan reality checks annoy him, but Wei is too valuable to dismiss
- **Chad:** His "Dwight" - Chad worships him, Jovanny encourages this because it feels good
- **Marcus:** Desperately wants Marcus to think he's cool. Marcus does not think he's cool.
- **Linda (HR):** Groans when she appears, "Oh God what now"
- **Sarah:** Respects her but doesn't really listen to customer feedback she brings
- **Zoe:** Weirdly patient with her mistakes, protective

### Comedy Style
- **Oblivious cringe** - Doesn't realize he's being inappropriate
- **Misplaced confidence** - Absolutely certain about things he's wrong about
- **Try-hard energy** - The harder he tries to be cool, the less cool he becomes
- **Earnest delusion** - Genuinely believes his own hype

### Example Scenarios
- Derailing standup to discuss Byzantine Empire (started looking at user geography)
- Getting defensive when Wei points out a product flaw
- Trying to show Marcus his deadlift form in office
- Announcing fourth "top priority" of the week
- Not understanding why people don't want to work weekends ("But we're building something special!")
- Talking head about height: "Yeah I'm 6'4", people always mention it, it's like... yeah I know"

## 2. WEI CHEN - Senior Engineer
**Office Archetype:** Jim Halpert

### Basic Info
- **Age:** 28
- **Role:** Senior Full-Stack Engineer (local, in SF)
- **Physical Description for Sora:**
  - Height: 5'9", slim build
  - Asian American, clean-cut
  - Glasses (black frame, modern)
  - Typical outfit: Hoodies (gray, black, navy), t-shirts with tech company logos, jeans
  - Posture: Relaxed, often leaning back in chair
  - Expression: Default is deadpan/slight smirk

### Core Blind Spots
1. **Burned Out But Won't Leave** - Stays out of inertia, golden handcuffs
2. **Secretly Cares More Than He Admits** - Acts like he doesn't care but actually does
3. **Avoids Conflict** - Uses sarcasm instead of direct confrontation

### Verbal Tics
- Delivers absurd statements completely deadpan
- "That's... great" (when it's not great)
- "Cool cool cool" (when he's checked out)
- "I mean... sure" (resigned agreement)
- Trails off mid-sentence when he realizes no one's listening

### Physical Signature
- **The Jim Look:** Direct camera look, eyebrows slightly raised, slight head tilt, "can you believe this" expression
- Slow turns to camera
- Minimal movement - the stillness is funny
- Types while obviously not listening to meetings
- The long blink of someone who's given up

### Key Relationships
- **Jovanny:** Tolerates him, provides reality checks that are ignored, has mastered the art of "yes and" (agreeing while clearly thinking it's dumb)
- **Raj:** Rivalry with remote engineer, competitive about code quality, bugs, who gets credit
- **Chad:** Finds him exhausting, avoids eye contact
- **Sarah:** Workplace friendship, both are the "sane ones," trade looks during meetings
- **Marcus:** Finds his hustle energy tiring but kind of respects it
- **Zoe:** Baffled that she gets away with everything

### Comedy Style
- **Deadpan reactions** - Says wild things with zero expression change
- **The camera confidant** - His looks are the audience's voice
- **Resigned sarcasm** - Not mean, just... tired
- **Visual stillness** - His lack of reaction IS the reaction

### Example Scenarios
- Camera look when Jovanny announces third pivot
- Talking head: "Last week we were an AI company. This week it's blockchain. Next week, who knows. Maybe we'll sell beets."
- Rivalry with Raj over whose code caused bug
- Caught on Zoom call obviously playing video game
- The only one who notices Zoe's disasters but stays quiet
- Asked to work weekend, long camera look before "...sure"

## 3. RAJ PATEL - Remote Engineer (India)
**Office Archetype:** Mix of Dwight's intensity + Andy's try-hard energy

### Basic Info
- **Age:** 26
- **Role:** Full-Stack Engineer (remote from Bangalore, India)
- **Physical Description for Sora:**
  - Indian, slim build
  - Short dark hair, clean-shaven
  - Glasses (sometimes)
  - Typical outfit: Button-up shirts even on Zoom (overdressed for startup), visible in small Zoom window
  - Background: Modest apartment, visible bookshelf
  - Energy: Earnest, sits up straight, leans toward camera

### Core Blind Spots
1. **Tries Too Hard to Prove He's As Good As Local Team** - Overcompensates
2. **Takes Everything Literally** - Misses sarcasm, especially Wei's
3. **Defensive About Remote Work** - Sensitive to any perceived slight about being remote

### Verbal Tics
- "Actually..." (correcting people)
- "As I mentioned in my previous message..." (annoyed people don't read)
- "It's 2am here but that's fine" (passive-aggressive about time zones)
- "The code clearly shows..." (being pedantic)
- Formal English, slight Indian accent

### Physical Signature
- **The Laptop Frame:** Often appears as just a face in a laptop screen while others are in room together
- Leans into camera when making a point
- Raises hand on Zoom (everyone else just talks)
- Screen share presentations that are over-prepared
- Muted but clearly talking (forgot to unmute)

### Key Relationships
- **Wei:** His rival. Competitive about code quality, bugs, features. Gets annoyed Wei gets more credit for being local.
- **Jovanny:** Wants validation from him, doesn't get it as much as Chad does
- **Chad:** Confused by his metrics obsession
- **Bob:** Raj often has to fix Bob's technical mistakes, resigned to this
- **Zoe:** Extremely patient explaining technical things to her (more patient than with Wei)

### Comedy Style
- **Earnest overachiever** - Tries so hard it becomes funny
- **Time zone martyrdom** - "It's 2am but I'm here"
- **The remote struggle** - Technical difficulties, always slightly delayed
- **Pedantic precision** - Corrects minor things everyone else ignores

### Example Scenarios
- Meeting where everyone's in room, Raj is just a laptop on the table
- Wei and Raj arguing over who caused bug, getting heated
- Raj's over-prepared presentation vs everyone else's casual updates
- "You're on mute, Raj" (classic)
- Talking head at 2am, clearly exhausted: "It's fine, this is what teamwork is"
- Celebrating fix he made while everyone else was asleep, no one acknowledges it
