# SaaS Scenario Library

This is your scenario database. Pick situations that resonate universally in SaaS/startup culture or are specific to QuickPing. Each scenario includes why it's funny and which characters would shine.

## PRODUCT & TECHNICAL SCENARIOS

### The Failed Demo
**Situation:** Product demo breaks during crucial moment (investor pitch, customer call, all-hands)
**Why it's funny:** The contrast between confidence and catastrophic failure
**Characters:** Jovanny (overconfident), Wei (warned him), Raj ("it works on my machine"), Bob (maybe his "fix" broke it)
**Potential beats:**
- Build confidence before the crash
- Immediate technical failure
- Desperate troubleshooting
- Someone saying "I told you to test it"
- Talking head afterward pretending it went fine

### It Works On My Machine
**Situation:** Code works perfectly locally, breaks in production
**Why it's funny:** Universal developer experience, the excuses
**Characters:** Wei vs Raj (arguing whose environment is "right"), Bob (doesn't understand), Chad (just wants it to work for metrics)
**Potential beats:**
- Confident deployment
- Immediate production errors
- "Well it works on MY machine"
- Investigation revealing absurd environment difference
- Someone (probably Raj) fixing it at 2am

**Using This Library:**

1. Pick a scenario that fits your character pairing
2. Identify the 1-3 specific beats to hit
3. Adapt to 10 or 30 second structure
4. Add QuickPing-specific visuals where relevant
5. Stay true to character blind spots and relationships

Mix and match elements across scenarios. Many of these can be combined or have QuickPing platform aspects added (showing things in Slack channels, portfolios, etc.).
