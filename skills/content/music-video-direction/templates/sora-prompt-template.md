# Sora Prompt Template for Music Videos

## Quick Fill Template

Use this template to structure your Sora prompts for music video clips.

```
A [SETTING/ENVIRONMENT] at [TIME/LIGHTING CONDITION]; [SHOT TYPE] of [SUBJECT] [ACTION/POSE]; [LENS CHOICE], [CAMERA MOVEMENT]; [LIGHTING DESCRIPTION]; [COLOR PALETTE/GRADING]; At 0:00: [INITIAL STATE]; At 0:03: [FIRST DEVELOPMENT]; At 0:06: [PROGRESSION/PEAK]; At 0:09: [CONCLUSION]. [AUDIO CUES]. [EXCLUSIONS].
```

## Example Filled Templates

### Example 1: Surreal/Conceptual

**Setting:** Infinite white void with no visible walls or horizon
**Shot:** Extreme wide shot
**Subject:** Silhouetted figure sitting cross-legged, floating 10 feet above nothing
**Lens:** 35mm
**Camera:** Slow 360-degree orbit
**Lighting:** Soft directionless diffused lighting creating subtle shadow below figure
**Color:** Completely desaturated, pure black and white
**At 0:00:** Figure suspended perfectly still in meditation pose
**At 0:03:** Figure begins slow rotation on their axis
**At 0:06:** Rotation speed increases slightly, hair begins to float upward
**At 0:09:** Figure completes full rotation, returns to stillness
**Audio:** Ambient hum at 0:00, pitch rises from 0:03-0:09
**Exclusions:** No floor visible, maintain perfect void, no lens flare

**COMPLETE PROMPT:**
```
An infinite white void with no visible walls, floor, or horizon; extreme wide shot of silhouetted figure floating cross-legged 10 feet in empty space; 35mm lens, slow 360-degree orbit around subject; soft directionless diffused lighting creating subtle shadow projection below figure; completely desaturated pure black and white; At 0:00: figure suspended perfectly still in meditation pose; At 0:03: figure begins slow rotation on their own axis; At 0:06: rotation speed increases slightly, hair floats upward; At 0:09: completes full rotation, returns to stillness. Ambient hum rises in pitch from 0:03 to 0:09. No floor visible, maintain perfect void, no lens flare.
```

### Example 2: Performance/Artist

**Shot:** Medium shot
**Subject:** Singer in leather jacket, holding vintage microphone
**Lens:** 50mm, shallow depth of field
**Camera:** Slow push in

**Exclusions:** No background elements visible, maintain pure black void beyond spotlight, avoid face distortion

**COMPLETE PROMPT:**
```
A dark empty stage with single spotlight illuminating circular area; medium shot of singer in black leather jacket holding vintage dynamic microphone; 50mm lens with shallow depth of field, slow dolly push in toward subject; dramatic single spotlight from above creating hard shadows, everything else in pure darkness; high-contrast with deep blacks and warm golden spotlight; At 0:00: singer stands still, head down, spotlight dim; At 0:03: slowly raises head, spotlight brightens; At 0:06: begins singing motion, eyes connect with camera; At 0:09: spotlight at full intensity, singer fully engaged in performance. Instrumental atmosphere at 0:00, vocal begins at 0:06. No background elements visible, maintain pure black void beyond spotlight, natural skin tones.
```

### Example 3: Narrative

## Quick Checklist Before Generating

- [ ] Prompt is 50-100 words
- [ ] Specific setting described
- [ ] Shot type explicitly stated
- [ ] Lens choice included
- [ ] Camera movement specified
- [ ] Lighting described
- [ ] Color palette/grading mentioned
- [ ] 4 time-stamped beats (0:00, 0:03, 0:06, 0:09)
- [ ] Exclusions stated
- [ ] One clear action arc (not too much happening)
- [ ] Audio cues if needed
- [ ] Realistic for 10 seconds

---

## Tips for Success

**Specificity wins:** "A rainy Tokyo alley" > "A city street"
**Realistic timing:** Can this actually happen in 10 seconds?
**Consistent tone:** Don't mix conflicting directions
**Embrace constraints:** One idea per clip, executed well
**Plan continuity:** How does this connect to previous/next clip?
