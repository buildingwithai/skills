# Color Grading and Mood Systems

## Understanding Color in Music Videos

Color is emotion. Every color choice affects how viewers feel, often subconsciously. For Sora prompts, precise color descriptions create consistent, emotionally resonant results.

## Color Theory Fundamentals

### Primary Color Emotions

**RED**
- **Emotions:** Passion, anger, danger, love, intensity, energy, urgency
- **Physical response:** Increases heart rate, creates urgency
- **When to use:** Love songs, aggressive music, passion, warning, intensity
- **Avoid when:** Calm, peaceful, sad moments
- **Sora prompt:** "Deep red color palette" or "Crimson and scarlet tones"

**BLUE**
- **Emotions:** Calm, sadness, cold, distance, trust, stability, melancholy
- **Physical response:** Slows heart rate, creates calm or sadness
- **When to use:** Sad songs, calm moments, distance, reflection, ocean/sky themes
- **Avoid when:** Warmth, energy, passion needed
- **Sora prompt:** "Cool blue color palette" or "Deep azure and navy tones"

**YELLOW**
- **Emotions:** Joy, happiness, optimism, energy, youth, warmth
- **Physical response:** Uplifting, energizing
- **When to use:** Happy songs, optimistic moments, sunrise, youth
- **Avoid when:** Sadness, seriousness, darkness needed
- **Sora prompt:** "Bright yellow color palette" or "Golden yellow tones"

**GREEN**
- **Emotions:** Nature, growth, envy, unease (depending on shade), renewal, peace
- **Physical response:** Calming or unsettling depending on shade
- **When to use:** Nature themes, growth, renewal OR unease, sickness
- **Shades matter:** Sage green (peaceful) vs neon green (toxic/unease)
- **Sora prompt:** "Natural green color palette" or "Toxic neon green tones"

**ORANGE**
- **Emotions:** Energy, enthusiasm, creativity, warmth, adventure
- **Physical response:** Energizing, warm
- **When to use:** Energetic songs, warmth, creativity, sunset
- **Sora prompt:** "Warm orange color palette" or "Burnt orange and amber tones"

**PURPLE/VIOLET**
- **Emotions:** Luxury, mystery, spirituality, creativity, royalty, magic
- **Physical response:** Intrigue, contemplation
- **When to use:** Mysterious themes, luxury, spirituality, night
- **Sora prompt:** "Deep purple color palette" or "Violet and lavender tones"

**BLACK**
- **Emotions:** Mystery, power, elegance, death, sophistication, void
- **Physical response:** Dramatic, mysterious
- **When to use:** Dark themes, elegance, power, emptiness
- **Sora prompt:** "Predominantly black with..." or "Deep blacks"

**WHITE**
- **Emotions:** Purity, emptiness, peace, sterility, void, new beginning
- **Physical response:** Clean, stark
- **When to use:** Minimalism, purity, emptiness, sterile environments
- **Sora prompt:** "Pure white color palette" or "Stark white with..."

## Popular Music Video Color Grades

### 1. Teal and Orange (Cinematic Popular)

**Description:** Cool teal/cyan in shadows and backgrounds, warm orange in skin tones and highlights

**Emotion:** Cinematic, modern, polished, professional

**Why it works:** Complementary colors, skin tones pop, feels expensive

**When to use:**
- Professional polished look
- Modern pop, R&B, hip-hop
- Want cinematic quality
- Daytime or golden hour scenes

**Sora prompt:**
```
Cinematic teal and orange color grade, cool teal cyan in shadows and backgrounds, warm orange amber in highlights and skin tones, high contrast, polished look
```

**Variations:**
- Heavy teal and orange: Strong contrast, obvious grading
- Subtle teal and orange: Natural but enhanced
- Vintage teal and orange: Add grain, reduce saturation

### 2. Desaturated Cool Tones

**Description:** Muted, desaturated colors with blue/gray cast

**Emotion:** Melancholy, sadness, coldness, depression, distance, indie aesthetic

**Why it works:** Removes warmth = removes joy, creates emotional distance

**When to use:**
- Sad ballads
- Indie/alternative music
- Emotional distance themes
- Winter, coldness, isolation

**Sora prompt:**
```
Desaturated cool blue-gray color palette, muted colors with minimal saturation, cold blue cast throughout, melancholic mood, overcast atmosphere
```

### 3. Warm Vintage/Nostalgic

**Description:** Warm golden tones, slight fading, possible film grain

**Emotion:** Nostalgia, warmth, memory, 70s/80s feel, romance

**Why it works:** Mimics aged film, triggers nostalgia response

**When to use:**
- Nostalgic themes
- Vintage aesthetics
- Warm romantic moments
- Retro genres

**Sora prompt:**
```
Warm vintage color grade with golden yellow cast, slightly faded colors, sepia undertones, nostalgic film aesthetic, slight grain texture
```

### 4. High Contrast Black and White

**Description:** Pure black and white with strong contrast

**Emotion:** Classic, timeless, dramatic, stark, artistic

**Why it works:** Removes color distraction, emphasizes light/shadow, form

**When to use:**
- Artistic statements
- Timeless classic feel
- Focus on performance/emotion
- Indie/alternative aesthetic

**Sora prompt:**
```
High-contrast black and white, pure monochrome, deep blacks and bright whites, no color, classic film aesthetic, strong tonal contrast
```

### 5. Pastel Dream

**Description:** Soft pastel colors (pink, lavender, mint, peach)

**Emotion:** Dreamy, soft, romantic, ethereal, youthful, fantasy

**Why it works:** Soft colors feel gentle, dreamlike, safe

**When to use:**
- Dream sequences
- Soft pop, indie pop
- Romance, gentleness
- Youth, innocence

**Sora prompt:**
```
Soft pastel color palette, gentle pink lavender mint and peach tones, low saturation, dreamy ethereal atmosphere, cotton candy colors
```

### 6. Neon/Cyberpunk

**Description:** Saturated neon colors (pink, blue, purple) often with dark backgrounds

**Emotion:** Future, energy, nightlife, technology, edgy

**Why it works:** High contrast, attention-grabbing, modern

**When to use:**
- Electronic/EDM music
- Night scenes
- Urban/city themes
- Futuristic concepts

**Sora prompt:**
```
Saturated neon color palette, electric pink blue and purple neon lights, dark backgrounds, cyberpunk aesthetic, high contrast neon glow
```

### 7. Monochromatic Single Color

**Description:** Entire video in shades of one color

**Emotion:** Depends on color chosen, artistic, focused, intense

**Why it works:** Unified aesthetic, bold choice, memorable

**When to use:**
- Strong artistic statement
- Specific emotional focus
- Conceptual videos

**Sora prompt:**
```
Monochromatic red color scheme, entire scene in varying shades of red from deep crimson to pale pink, unified single-color aesthetic
```

### 8. Natural Realistic

**Description:** Colors as they appear in real life, minimal grading

**Emotion:** Authentic, raw, documentary, honest

**Why it works:** Feels real, unmanipulated, trustworthy

**When to use:**
- Documentary-style
- Raw authentic moments
- Singer-songwriter intimate videos
- When honesty is key

**Sora prompt:**
```
Natural realistic color palette, colors as they appear in real life, minimal grading, authentic look, true-to-life tones
```

## Mood-Based Color Palettes

### ISOLATION
**Colors:** Cool desaturated blues and grays with single warm element
**Example:** Gray-blue environment, person in muted warm clothing
**Sora prompt:** "Desaturated blue-gray palette with single warm orange element creating isolation"

### ANXIETY
**Colors:** Clashing colors, green-yellow sickly tones, harsh contrasts
**Example:** Sickly green lighting, harsh orange accents
**Sora prompt:** "Unsettling yellow-green color palette with harsh contrasts creating anxiety"

### JOY
**Colors:** Bright saturated warm colors (yellow, orange, bright blue)
**Example:** Sunny yellows, sky blues, vibrant
**Sora prompt:** "Bright saturated warm color palette, vibrant yellows and sky blues, joyful energy"

### MELANCHOLY
**Colors:** Desaturated cool tones, blues, grays, muted purples
**Example:** Rainy day blues, gray overcast
**Sora prompt:** "Desaturated cool blue and gray palette, muted purple undertones, melancholic mood"

### PASSION/LOVE
**Colors:** Warm reds, pinks, golds, oranges
**Example:** Rose pinks, deep reds, golden highlights
**Sora prompt:** "Warm romantic color palette, deep reds and rose pinks, golden amber highlights"

### POWER
**Colors:** Bold high-contrast (black and red, black and gold)
**Example:** Deep blacks with electric red or royal gold
**Sora prompt:** "High-contrast color palette, deep blacks with electric red accents, powerful bold look"

### FREEDOM
**Colors:** Bright open colors, sky blues, whites, airy pastels
**Example:** Open sky blues, white clouds, light
**Sora prompt:** "Bright airy color palette, sky blues and whites, light pastel accents, open free feeling"

### MYSTERY
**Colors:** Deep purples, dark blues, blacks with violet accents
**Example:** Night purples, deep navy, shadowy
**Sora prompt:** "Mysterious deep purple and navy color palette, dark shadows with violet accents"

## Genre-Specific Color Approaches

### HIP-HOP
**Common palettes:**
- High-contrast with bold saturated colors
- Teal and orange
- Neon on dark
- Golden/champagne tones (luxury)

**Sora prompt example:** "High-contrast teal and orange cinematic grade, bold saturated colors, luxury golden accents"

### POP
**Common palettes:**
- Bright saturated colors
- Pastel dream
- Rainbow/multi-color
- Clean bright whites and colors

**Sora prompt example:** "Bright saturated rainbow color palette, vibrant multiple colors, clean high-key lighting"

### ROCK
**Common palettes:**
- Desaturated with selective color
- High-contrast black and white
- Dark moody with blue cast
- Natural with slight desat

**Sora prompt example:** "Desaturated moody palette with cool blue cast, high contrast, dark shadows"

### R&B
**Common palettes:**
- Warm golden tones
- Teal and orange (subtle)
- Moody with warm highlights
- Vintage warm

**Sora prompt example:** "Warm golden color grade, soft amber highlights, intimate teal shadows, romantic mood"

### INDIE
**Common palettes:**
- Desaturated naturals
- Black and white
- Warm vintage
- Muted pastels

**Sora prompt example:** "Desaturated natural colors, muted earth tones, slight warm cast, indie aesthetic"

### ELECTRONIC/EDM
**Common palettes:**
- Neon cyberpunk
- High saturation
- Ultraviolet and neon
- Technicolor bright

**Sora prompt example:** "Saturated neon color palette, electric pink blue purple, cyberpunk night aesthetic"

## Color Transitions and Evolution

### Static Palette
Same colors throughout entire video
**When:** Unified aesthetic, specific mood throughout

### Evolving Palette
Colors shift throughout video
**Example:** Start cool/desaturated, warm up to bright/saturated

### Section-Based Color
Different song sections = different colors
**Example:**
- Verse: Cool blues
- Chorus: Warm reds/oranges
- Bridge: Purple/different

### Emotional Journey Color
Colors follow emotional arc
**Example:** Sadness (blue) → Hope (adding warmth) → Joy (golden)

## Time of Day and Color

### GOLDEN HOUR (Sunrise/Sunset)
**Natural colors:** Warm golden oranges, soft pinks, long shadows
**Emotion:** Beauty, nostalgia, romance, magic
**Sora prompt:** "Golden hour lighting, warm orange and pink tones, soft golden glow, long shadows"

### BLUE HOUR (Dawn/Dusk)
**Natural colors:** Deep blues, purples, soft light
**Emotion:** Mystery, transition, contemplation
**Sora prompt:** "Blue hour lighting, deep blue and purple twilight tones, soft ambient glow"

### MIDDAY (Harsh Sun)
**Natural colors:** Bright, harsh shadows, high contrast, saturated
**Emotion:** Energy, harshness, reality, intensity
**Sora prompt:** "Harsh midday sunlight, bright high-contrast lighting, strong shadows, saturated colors"

### OVERCAST
**Natural colors:** Soft, diffused, muted, gray-blue cast
**Emotion:** Melancholy, softness, calm, sadness
**Sora prompt:** "Overcast diffused lighting, soft gray-blue cast, muted colors, gentle shadows"

### NIGHT (Artificial Light)
**Artificial colors:** Neon, streetlights (orange), moonlight (blue-white)
**Emotion:** Mystery, urban, intimacy, danger
**Sora prompt:** "Night scene, neon lights creating color pools, deep shadows, urban nighttime palette"

## Practical Sora Prompt Color Specifications

### Format 1: Descriptive Palette
```
[Primary color] and [secondary color] color palette
```
Example: "Teal and orange color palette"

### Format 2: Mood-Based
```
[Mood] color grade with [specific tones]
```
Example: "Melancholic color grade with desaturated blue-gray tones"

### Format 3: Technical Grading
```
[Saturation level] [color temperature] color palette, [specific characteristics]
```
Example: "Highly saturated warm color palette, vibrant oranges and yellows, punchy contrast"

### Format 4: Reference-Based
```
[Film/era/style] color aesthetic
```
Example: "1970s vintage film aesthetic, warm faded colors"

### Format 5: Detailed Breakdown
```
[Background colors], [subject/highlight colors], [shadow colors], [overall mood]
```
Example: "Cool teal backgrounds, warm orange skin tones and highlights, deep blue shadows, cinematic mood"

## Color Combinations That Work

**Complementary (Opposite on Color Wheel):**
- Red and Green (Christmas, but use carefully)
- Blue and Orange (Classic cinematic)
- Purple and Yellow (Vibrant contrast)

**Analogous (Next to Each Other):**
- Blue, Blue-Green, Green (Calm, natural)
- Red, Orange, Yellow (Warm energy)
- Purple, Blue, Blue-Green (Cool mystery)

**Triadic (Evenly Spaced):**
- Red, Yellow, Blue (Primary vibrant)
- Orange, Green, Purple (Bold artistic)

## Testing Your Color Choices

**Questions to ask:**
- [ ] Does this color palette match the song's emotion?
- [ ] Does it fit the genre conventions (or intentionally subvert them)?
- [ ] Will it look good for 3+ minutes? (Not too jarring)
- [ ] Does it differentiate from other videos in this genre?
- [ ] Can I describe it clearly in a Sora prompt?

## Common Color Mistakes

❌ **Too many colors** (rainbow vomit with no purpose)
✅ **2-4 main colors max**

❌ **Colors fight the emotion** (bright happy colors for sad song)
✅ **Colors support emotion**

❌ **Vague prompt** ("nice colors")
✅ **Specific prompt** ("Desaturated blue-gray with warm orange highlights")

❌ **Generic** (default camera colors)
✅ **Intentional** (chosen and described palette)
