# Content Scorecard

Score any idea or concept on contentability:

## Dimensions (1-10 each)

**1. Visual Interest**
- Can you SHOW this in first 3 seconds?
- 10 = Extremely visual, 1 = Abstract concept

**2. Story Potential**
- Is there a narrative arc (beginning/middle/end)?
- 10 = Compelling story, 1 = Static information

**3. Hook Strength**
- Will it stop the scroll?
- 10 = Provocative/shocking, 1 = Generic

**4. Shareability**
- Why would someone share this?
- 10 = "Everyone needs to see this", 1 = "Cool I guess"

**5. Repeatability**
- Can this become a series?
- 10 = Infinite episodes, 1 = One-off only

## Calculate

Total: ___ / 50

**Scoring:**
- 40-50: Extremely contentable → Proceed
- 30-39: Good potential → Test
- 20-29: Needs work → Iterate
- <20: Not contentable → Abandon or pivot

## Compare Ideas

Use this scorecard to compare multiple ideas side-by-side.
Winner = Highest total score.
