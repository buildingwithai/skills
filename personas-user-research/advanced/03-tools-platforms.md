# Tools & Platforms for Persona Creation

Comparison of persona creation tools and when to use them.

## Free Tools

### HubSpot Make My Persona

**What it is:** Free guided persona generator

**Features:**
- 7-stage question workflow
- Instant downloadable PDF
- Professional template
- 100% free

**Best for:**
- First-time persona creators
- Limited budget
- Need professional format quickly

**Limitations:**
- Not collaborative (one person at a time)
- Fixed template (limited customization)
- No analytics integration

**How to use:**
1. Visit: hubspot.com/make-my-persona
2. Answer 7 stages of questions
3. Download formatted PDF

### UserPersona.dev

**What it is:** Free AI-powered persona generator

**Features:**
- AI generates personas from prompts
- Quick generation (minutes)
- Export options

**Best for:**
- Rapid prototyping
- Getting started quickly
- Proto-personas

**Limitations:**
- AI-generated (needs human validation)
- No team collaboration
- Basic templates

### Miro Customer Persona Template

**What it is:** Collaborative whiteboard template

**Features:**
- Visual, sticky-note based
- Real-time collaboration
- Free with Miro account
- Workshop-friendly

**Best for:**
- Team workshops
- Remote collaboration
- Visual thinkers
- Iterative development

**Limitations:**
- Requires Miro account
- Less structured than dedicated tools
- Manual formatting for sharing

## Paid Tools

### Xtensio ($14/month)

**What it is:** Visual persona builder with AI assist

**Features:**
- AI-powered insights
- Beautiful visual templates
- Team collaboration
- Export to PDF/web
- Up to 5 personas free, unlimited paid

**Best for:**
- Teams wanting polish
- Multiple personas
- Presenting to stakeholders

**Cost:** $14/month (or $8/month annual)

**Limitations:**
- Learning curve for advanced features
- 5 persona limit on free tier

### Delve.ai (Enterprise pricing)

**What it is:** Automated AI personas from analytics

**Features:**
- Generates personas from website analytics
- Real-time data updates
- Behavioral segmentation
- Competitor persona analysis

**Best for:**
- Large user bases (10,000+)
- Companies with rich analytics data
- Continuous persona updates

**Cost:** Custom enterprise pricing

**Limitations:**
- Expensive
- Requires significant data
- AI-generated (needs validation)

### UXPressia ($25/month)

**What it is:** Journey mapping + persona tool

**Features:**
- Persona creation + journey maps
- Team collaboration
- Export options
- Template library

**Best for:**
- UX teams
- Combining personas with journey mapping
- Visual storytelling

**Cost:** $25/month

## Choosing the Right Tool

### Decision Tree

**Need it free?**
→ HubSpot (structured) or Miro (collaborative)

**Running a workshop?**
→ Miro

**Want AI assistance?**
→ Xtensio ($14/mo) or UserPersona.dev (free)

**Have analytics data + budget?**
→ Delve.ai

**Need journey maps too?**
→ UXPressia

**Want polished visuals for stakeholders?**
→ Xtensio

## Template Downloads

### Free Templates

**Google Slides/PowerPoint:**
- Persona one-pager template
- Search: "free persona template PPT"

**Notion/Coda:**
- Persona database templates
- Community-shared persona boards

**Figma:**
- Persona design templates
- Free in Figma Community

### What to Include in Template

**Essential fields:**
- Name & photo
- Role & company context
- Goals (3-5)
- Pain points (3-5)
- Behaviors
- Decision criteria
- Quote

**Optional fields:**
- Demographics
- Day in the life
- Information sources
- Tech stack

## Do You Need a Tool?

**When tools help:**
- Team collaboration required
- Need professional formatting
- Presenting to stakeholders
- Managing multiple personas

**When Google Docs is fine:**
- Solo persona development
- Internal use only
- Limited budget
- Iterating quickly

**Truth:** The tool matters less than the research and thinking
